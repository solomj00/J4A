Hi, I'm Joe and I'm running for Select Board!

Arlington is more than just a zip code to me. It is the town that raised me, the community where I chose to build my life, and the place where I am now raising my own children. My connection to this town is not just sentimental; it is practical and deeply rooted.

I've navigated Arlington as a student in our public schools, as a homeowner, as a parent leading the Dallin PTO, as a son helping my family age in place, and as a member of town government.

I am running for office because I want to ensure that Arlington remains a place of opportunity for every generation—from the students entering kindergarten to the seniors who have spent a lifetime building this community.
